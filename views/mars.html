Welcome to Mars!

Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun and is the second-smallest planet in the Solar System, larger than only Mercury. In English, Mars is the name of the Roman god of war and is often referred to as the "Red Planet". The Red Planet designation refers to the effect of the iron oxide prevalent on Mars's surface, which gives it the distinctive reddish appearance, unique among the astronomical bodies visible to the naked eye.
